## Writing Sample Preview

The demo subchapter — *"Capital Structure and Firm Value: Reassessing the Trade-Off Framework in the Post-Crisis Era"* — covers:

- **Modigliani–Miller irrelevance theorem** and its analytical role
- **Static trade-off theory** (Kraus & Litzenberger, 1973) with critical assessment of empirical support
- **Pecking-order theory** (Myers & Majluf, 1984) and its limitations
- **Post-crisis developments** — financial flexibility as a first-order determinant (DeAngelo & DeAngelo, 2007)
- **Research design implications** — control variables and dynamic leverage measures

**18 peer-reviewed sources** cited, including Modigliani & Miller (1958, 1963), Frank & Goyal (2009), Graham & Leary (2011), and Fama & French (2005).
