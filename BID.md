# BID — Master's Dissertation in Corporate Finance



### Questions to ask after project is awarded
- What citation style does the university require? (Harvard, APA 7, Chicago?)
- Is there a specific word count target? (15,000? 20,000?)
- Does the university require an ethics approval for data use?
- Does the student have access to financial databases (Bloomberg, WRDS, Orbis)?
- Is there a supervisor who will review interim drafts?
- What is the absolute deadline for submission?
- Are there interim deadlines (e.g. proposal defence, draft chapter reviews)?
